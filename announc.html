Announcements !


  • COVID-19 Precautionary Norms :

    As per government norms, students coming to campus must produce COVID-19 negative certificate from authorized agencies. Also, parents' consent letter is mandatory. Both these documents have to be submitted to the department Chaiperson without fail. Copy (soft copy on the phone is acceptable) of these documents should be with students at all times while on campus and should be shown to any University authority when asked. Please wear face mask covering mouth and nose, carry personal hand sanitizer and maintain social distancing whenever you step out of your residence and while you are on campus


  • End Semester Examination details :

    The end semester examination for odd semesters (1/3/5) of undergraduate courses (B.Tech/ B.Com) will be in offline mode & would start from January 7th, 2021. Detailed schedule for regular, backlog & improvement examination will be released from the office. If there is any discrepancy , please let your departmenthead know immediately.


  • Chairman Addressing :

    The Chairman of Exceed group of Institutions will be addressing all B.Tech students in light of recent technical advancements that have been made in labs. The addressing will be held on 12 th March, 11:00 am in V S Acharya Hall. Make sure to attend the addressing.


  • Cultural Events :

    As part of the college festival "Virasat", cultural events will be held for students as well as teachers. More details will be announced later.
